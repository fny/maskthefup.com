MASK MYTHS DEBUNKED

Shedding light on some of the falsehoods being spread around.

MASKS DO NOT REDUCE OXYGEN LEVELS

There are several videos online that show there is no significant or harmful reduction in blood oxygenation from wearing masks for extended periods. Remember health professionals and industrial workers often wear these masks for hours on end without a problem.

Wearing a mask for an hour in the grocery store definitely won't cause you any problems. In fact, you can go for a run without any issues (we've done it!) Wearing a mask is uncomfortable though if you're not used to it. Perhaps walk away from everyone, and take a break!

MASKS DO NOT CAUSE HARMFUL CO2 LEVELS

There are several videos online that show elevated CO2 levels when wearing a mask. While these videos are, in fact, correct, there is no detrimental effect to ones health or immunity because oxygen saturation levels remain adequate!

Don't take our word for it! Dr. Megan Hall, a physician in South Carolina, has shown it on her Facebook page. Healthline also has a wonderful article entitled "No, Face Masks Can't Cause CO2 Poisoning" that's worth a read.

MASKS ARE NOT POLITICAL

We all need to wear masks to make America stronger, to prepare ourselves to reopen our economy, and to prevent further shutdowns. Other countries have done this, we can too if we work together!

MASKS ARE NOT SHARIA LAW

So there's a claim that face masks are a sign that muslims are taking over the country and mask wearing is a way to acclimate us to their beliefs. This is absolute nonsense.

CLOTH MASKS DO NOT PROVIDE COMPLETE PROTECTION

Cloth masks do not provide complete protection. You still must social distance. The reason they are promoted is that the CDC does not want resources to be diverted from healthcare workers.

Be extremely cautious while wearing a cloth mask. Better yet, purchase medical-grade masks. Supply has now reached levels where its fine to purchase one for non-medical use.

YOU CANNOT REUSE SURGICAL AND N95 MASKS

If you wear any of these masks directly on your face, you should not reuse them. They can easily become carriers of disease if reused especially over multiple days.

If you work in the health profession, the strategy for reuse that we recommend is to wear a surgical mask underneath a N95 or KN95. This will allow you to reuse the N95 or KN95 while disposing the single use masks.

IF I'M NOT SICK I DON'T NEED TO WEAR A MASK

Not everyone who gets infected with the coronavirus gets sick. You might be a carrier.

Because we don’t know for sure who’s infected, the best option is for all of us to wear masks. Other countries have had success with this, so just put the damn thing on.
